*The Game Company has one or more Projects.
*Each project is assigned a unique identifier, a name, a launch or ship date, and a State.
*The Game Company hires employees.
*Each employee has a unique identifier, a first name, the last name, and a Job_ID which leads to the identification of their job role.
*Each employee is assigned to one and only one project.

Each Project State has a unique identifier and a description.

*Game programmers are a Job role.
*Game artists are Job role.
*Game designers are Job role.
*Producers are a Job role.
*Testers are an employee role.
